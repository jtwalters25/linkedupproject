    Link-Up

  • WHAT IS IT?
    This app helps you find meet-ups using data collected from your linkedIn profile when you log in.
  • HOW DOES IT WORK?
    Using a mash up of API's we are able to connect you to Meet-Ups using the data collected from your LinkedIn profile including you industry and location.
  • WHY DO I NEED IT?
    Online presence is never enough. You never know you could end up growing your network or landing a great job or business partner from face to face professional interactions.
  • WHO WILL I LINK UP WITH ?
    Meet up with potential business partners, employers or employees using a combination of LinkedIn and Meet Up.com.
  • WHERE DO WE LINK?
    Link Up at locations within your zip code that are listed by most recently posted.
